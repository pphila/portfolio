As for education I have a Bachelor's degree in Biology. I have worked many jobs from laborer and machine operator in a factory, Wholefoods market team member to Certified Nursing Assistant in a nursing home and hospital, and currently am still working at OHSU as a Head Unit Coordinator. I chose to enroll into this programming bootcamp because after working in the hospital setting throughout the pandemic really cemented that the medical career path was not for me. Though I still love learning about medicine, the real world aspect of the enduring abuse from patients, dealing with the rampant medical illiteracy, and outright disregard to scientific fact had accelerated the feeling of burnout. Understanding that getting into a medical school is still quite a ways out and the expense of applications aswell as the realization that any medical school is expensive, I chose to embark onto this career path instead, I'm excited and nervous but am very much happy for to be part of this bootcamp.
